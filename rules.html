Server Rules

1. Respect Everyone

Be respectful to all players. No harassment, hate speech, or bullying allowed.

2. No Griefing

Do not destroy or steal from other players' builds. Build on your own land or claim it.

3. No Cheating

Use of hacks, mods, or cheats is strictly prohibited. Fair play is required.

4. No Spam

Do not spam chat with excessive messages or links. Keep conversations appropriate.

5. Follow Server Guidelines

Read and follow all server-specific rules and guidelines provided by staff.

6. Have Fun!

The most important rule is to have fun and enjoy the server with others.
